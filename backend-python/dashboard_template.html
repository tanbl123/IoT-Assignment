How it was evaluated

Two safeguards keep these numbers honest:
  • Leakage-free split — overlapping windows from the same recording never appear in both training and test.
  • Subject-independent (LOSO) — trained on all-but-one subject, tested on the held-out person, rotated through everyone. Measures generalisation to new users.
